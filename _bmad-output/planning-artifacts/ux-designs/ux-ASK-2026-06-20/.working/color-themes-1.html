What differs between options (everything else is identical)

  • A: Espresso only in the nav bar. Daily Brief is a white card with a camel left-border accent. Lightest feel — most like an editorial magazine. No espresso fill anywhere in the content area.
  • B: Espresso nav + espresso Daily Brief header. The brief header serves as a deliberate "gravity anchor" for the page — draws the eye immediately. All other cards are white on linen. This is a refinement of your current approach.
  • C: Same as B but with khaki (#D7C9B8) card borders and a slightly deeper linen page background (#EDE8E0). Adds more visual structure and warmth without adding espresso fills. Feels most layered and premium.
  • Sentiment badge (all options): dot + label pill — satisfies WCAG 2.1 AA non-color indicator requirement.
  • Card hierarchy (all options): Headline + sentiment → AI impact → stocks → source/time/category footer (as per your preferred scan order).
