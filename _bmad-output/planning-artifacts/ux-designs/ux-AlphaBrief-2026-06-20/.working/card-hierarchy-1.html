Design notes for Option B

  • Sentiment badge (top-right of headline): uses dot + label — meets WCAG 2.1 AA non-color requirement. Color alone is insufficient for bearish/bullish distinction.
  • AI box moves from "optional detail" to "primary value prop" — positioned immediately after headline, before stock tickers.
  • Stock impacts stay above the fold but below the AI insight — they answer "which stocks?" after the "what happened?" and "so what?" are established.
  • Source + time + category demoted to footer row — these are credibility signals, not discovery signals. Users arrived at this card from a feed already filtered by category.
  • Featured cards (left camel border) are unchanged — that accent remains a visual hook for editors to promote items.
  • No inline summary text in Option B — the AI insight replaces the raw summary as the secondary text. This saves vertical space and eliminates the "why read both?" confusion.
  • Detail page: clicking navigates to a permalink. Summary, full AI analysis, all sector impacts, original source link, AI disclaimer, and analysis timestamp all live on the detail page only.
